The ritual

How to taste serious chocolate.

01Observe

Notice the color and natural sheen, without excessive polish.

02Break

Listen for the clean snap. A well-tempered bar sounds crisp.

03Melt

Let it soften on the palate without chewing.

04Discover

Wait. The flavor changes three times before it leaves.
