Why Pittahaya exists

We saw a digital gap — and decided to close it.

In highly digitalized markets almost every business has a website. Digital presence isn't a luxury anymore — it's a growth tool: it helps you reach more people, build trust, project professionalism, and create real connection with each client.

When someone wants to learn about a business today, the first thing they do is search it on Google. If they don't find clear, current, professional information, they lose interest or pick another option. In Ecuador, many great businesses still lose customers for this exact reason — not because their product is bad, but because their digital presence doesn't communicate everything they're worth.

Pittahaya exists to close that gap — to prove that any business, regardless of size, can have a modern, trustworthy image ready to grow.

Our mission

Our vision

Become a digital reference in Ecuador, Canada, and beyond.

Pittahaya was born from a personal experience: living and studying in Canada showed us up close how a fully digitalized market works — one where an online presence is essential for any business to grow. Comparing that reality to Ecuador, we saw the opportunity — and the responsibility — to help more businesses cross that same threshold. Today we're still guided by the same idea: every business deserves a digital presence that represents it, connects it with its clients, and prepares it to grow.
