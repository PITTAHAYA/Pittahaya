The story

From Ecuador to Canada — and back with a mission.

Living and studying in Canada showed me up close how a fully digitalized market works: there, a business without a good website barely exists. When I compared that reality with Ecuador's, I saw something I couldn't ignore — excellent businesses, with incredible products, losing customers simply because their digital presence didn't communicate everything they were worth.

That's how Pittahaya was born: to close that gap. To prove that any business, no matter its size or its city, deserves to look modern, trustworthy, and ready to compete — in Ecuador and beyond.

What we believe

A website isn't “something nice to look at.” It's your best salesperson: it works around the clock, builds trust from the first second, and turns a visitor into a client — even while you're resting.
