Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026. At Pittahaya we believe in clarity: here's exactly what data we collect, why, and how we protect it — no fine print.

1. Who we are

Pittahaya is a premium web design and AI automation studio. This policy applies to this website (pittahaya.com) and the forms and interactions we offer here. We are the controller of the data you share with us.

2. What data we collect

We only collect what you voluntarily give us through our forms (contact and free audit) or our chat:

  • Name.
  • Email address.
  • Your business or brand name (if provided).
  • Phone or contact method (if provided).
  • The service or plan you're interested in and the message you send us.
  • Minimal technical/security data (for example, to protect against spam via Cloudflare Turnstile).

We don't ask for sensitive information, and you don't need to create an account to contact us.

3. How we use your data

  • Reply to your message and give you a diagnosis or quote.
  • Follow up on your request and, if we move forward, manage your project.
  • Improve our service and prevent abuse or spam.

We don't sell your data or use it for third-party advertising. The basis for processing it is your consent when you submit the form and our legitimate interest in handling your request.

4. Who we share data with

We don't sell or trade your data. We only rely on trusted technology providers that help us operate, and only for that purpose:

  • Supabase — secure storage of form data.
  • Resend — delivery of confirmation and notification emails.
  • Cloudflare — spam and bot protection (Turnstile).
  • Vercel — website hosting.

These providers may process data outside Ecuador. We choose well-known services that apply industry-standard security measures.

5. Cookies and tracking

We don't use advertising or third-party tracking cookies. We only use technical and security technologies needed for the site to work and to protect it from abuse. If we add analytics in the future, we'll reflect it here.

6. How long we keep it

We keep your data for as long as needed to handle your request and, if we work together, for the duration of the business relationship and any period required by law. After that, we delete or anonymize it. You can ask us to delete it at any time.

7. Your rights

Under Ecuador's Organic Law on the Protection of Personal Data, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you.
  • Correct it if it's inaccurate.
  • Request its deletion.
  • Object to or withdraw your consent.

To exercise any of these rights, reach us through our contact form or WhatsApp. We reply within 24 hours.

8. Security

Your information always travels over an encrypted connection (HTTPS) and is stored with appropriate security measures. Still, no system is 100% infallible; we work to protect it responsibly.

9. Minors

Our services are aimed at businesses and adults. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect improvements or legal changes. The current version will always be published on this page with its update date.
