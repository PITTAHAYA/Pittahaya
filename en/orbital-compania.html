Timeline

Six years.

2021

The calculation on a napkin

Three thermal-control engineers calculate how much radiating surface it would take to dissipate a GPU rack in orbit. The number is smaller than they expected.

2022

ORB-01 · demonstrator

A 12U CubeSat with a single commercial GPU survives nine months and delivers the first inference result computed outside the atmosphere.

2023

Series A · USD 48M

Capital is raised to industrialize the node. Land is secured for the Quito station at an altitude of 4,180 meters.

2024

First complete plane

Eight ECU-1 nodes reach equatorial orbit. The constellation brings its first production customer online.

2025

Series B · USD 210M

Three operational planes, four ground stations, 28.8 petaflops deployed, and 99.98% availability.

2026 · now

Block II

Twelve more nodes and the first dedicated optical switch. The target: 100 petaflops in orbit before the end of 2027.
